Speech to Text


Speech-to-text is a type of software that effectively takes audio content and transcribes it into written words in a word processor or other display destination. This type of speech recognition software is extremely valuable to anyone who needs to generate a lot of written content without a lot of manual typing


Emotion Analysis


Analyzes and understands emotion, demographics and attention in most videos and images. It look for faces in footage and pass the facial features and expressions through our face analysis algorithms. It will return values for the 6 universal emotions, age, gender, and other useful meta data about the faces found.
